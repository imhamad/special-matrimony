About us

Marriage is one of the greatest blessings in a person’s life, and choosing your partner is one of the most important decisions that you will ever make. Everyone wants to share their joys and sorrows with someone special. The aim of our platform is to unite soul mates. We take immense care of your beliefs, values and needs while arranging matchmaking.

Special-Matrimony is an exclusive matchmaking app for singles. Here you will find your dream life partner, matching your search preferences. When we start a relationship, honesty, loyalty, and mutual understanding between two individuals and their families are required to coordinate.

We believe in transparency and honesty before starting any relationship. Special-Matrimony will serve as an excellent platform connecting people with various types of conditions such as autism, hard of hearing, deaf, visual impairment and those with additional support needs, who are trying to find that special someone. We seek to make a positive difference in the lives of people with special needs and empower them to find the perfect life partner to form a lifelong relationship.

Marriage is all about having a happy and positive future. So, this is one of the reasons we will be with here to support you with your search and to ensure you have a pleasant experience using our platform. We feel proud to be a medium to bring love and happiness into your life.
